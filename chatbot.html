Welcome

Welcome to the My Lib RefChat portal. We are ready to assist you with library-related inquiries 24/7 through the chat below.

Soalan Lazim (FAQ)

Apakah perkhidmatan yang disediakan? Kami menawarkan bantuan rujukan, pinjaman bahan, akses ke pangkalan data langganan, dan latihan literasi maklumat.
Bila waktu operasi? Perkhidmatan chat kami beroperasi 24 jam setiap hari melalui sistem Tawk.to.
Bagaimana mengakses e-resources? Anda boleh log masuk ke laman e-resources melalui akaun pelajar atau staf anda. Hubungi kami jika memerlukan panduan.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What services are provided? We offer reference assistance, book lending, access to subscription databases, and information literacy training.
What are your operating hours? Our chat service is available 24/7 via the Tawk.to system.
How to access e-resources? You can log in to the e-resources portal using your student or staff account. Contact us if you need guidance.
